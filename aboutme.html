Who is the person behind the lens?

Once upon a time, in the magical town of Westport, there lived a talented and passionate photographer named Maria Migrova, also known as Majka Em. Her gift was capturing the beauty and essence of families and individuals through the lens of her camera. What set Majka apart from the other photographers in the land was her unique approach to her craft. She believed that the key to creating stunning portraits was not just in capturing a person's appearance, but in revealing their true character and spirit. To achieve this, Majka took the time to get to know each of her clients and their families personally. With a gentle touch and a warm smile, she made sure that everyone felt comfortable and relaxed during their photo shoot. As she worked her magic, she created a space where children giggled, parents laughed, and everyone felt like they were part of a fairy tale. For Majka, photography was not just a job - it was her passion and her calling. And with every portrait she created, she brought a little bit of magic and wonder into the world. As the great photographer Paul Caponigro once said, "It's one thing to make a picture of what a person looks like, it's another thing to make a portrait of who they are." And with Majka Em behind the camera, you can be sure that your portrait will be a true reflection of your unique and beautiful self.
